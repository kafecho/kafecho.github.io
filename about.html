About me

Hi, my name is Guillaume (just call me “G”).

I am a freelance Software Engineer / Configuration Management ‘consultant’ based in Johannesburg (South Africa).

I hail from Martinique, a French speaking island in the Caribbean.

I am a big fan of Functional Programming (yay Scala) and DevOps (yay Ansible) but more importantly I like to keep things simple, easy to understand and easy to use.

I am currently working remotely for Snell Advanced Media, a cool media company based in the UK where I work on backend systems, Web Apps and deploy (rather complex) things with Ansible.

I’ve given numerous talks at conferences and Meetups on topics such as Scala, Ansible, Property-based testing, and more recently Machine Learning.

You can always find me on Twitter (@gbelrose) or on a various Slack teams (ZA Tech, Elm) or ping me at kafecho@gmail.com.
